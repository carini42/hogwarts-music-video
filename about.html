Hogwarts.
Castle

Hogwarts was founded around AD 993 by two wizards, Godric Gryffindor and Salazar Slytherin, and two witches, Helga Hufflepuff and Rowena Ravenclaw. Each of them contained an aspect of personality they wanted in new students. However, shortly after the school was founded, Slytherin argued with the other founders about blood purity. Slytherin wanted to admit only pureblood students, while the other three founders disagreed. Slytherin left school soon after secretly building the Chamber of Secrets. When his or her true heir, the Slytherin Heir, returned to school, he or she would be able to open the Chamber and release the monster there in order to purify the school of all Muggleborn students.

Hogwarts School is housed by Hogwarts Castle, a large castle located in an unknown location in Scotland, near the wizarding village of Hogsmeade. The castle has extensive grounds covered with grass and flowering shrubs and vegetable gardens, a lake, dense forest, several greenhouses and a Quidditch stadium. The castle also houses an owlery, which houses all the owls owned by the school and students. The three tallest towers are the Astronomy Tower, the Gryffindor Tower and the Ravenclaw Tower, respectively. There are 142 flights of stairs, which are known to move, in the castle, which was built on huge rocks over the lake. The castle is known for the many changes it has undergone to its structure over the years, as well as rebuilding itself after the Battle of Hogwarts.

The Hogwarts coat of arms shows the animal symbol of each house and its colors. These are, clockwise from top left: the Gryffindor lion, the Slytherin snake, the Ravenclaw eagle and the Hufflepuff badger. Some rooms in the school tend to move, as do the steps in the Grand Staircase. Albus Dumbledore once claimed that he did not know all of the castle's secrets, as the site witnessed centuries of ancient magic.

The school has a considerable amount of spells and enchantments around it so you can't find yourself by a muggle. Certain people cannot see the school, they only see ruins and various warning signs. Witches and wizards cannot Apparate or Disapparate at Hogwarts, except when the Headmaster removes the enchantment. Electricity and electronic devices don't work at Hogwarts. Radios, however, are an exception.
