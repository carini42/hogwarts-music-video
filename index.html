Hogwarts.
Castle

Hogwarts School is housed by Hogwarts Castle, a large castle located in an unknown location in Scotland, near the wizarding village of Hogsmeade. The castle has extensive grounds covered with grass and flowering shrubs and vegetable gardens, a lake, dense forest, several greenhouses and a Quidditch stadium.
